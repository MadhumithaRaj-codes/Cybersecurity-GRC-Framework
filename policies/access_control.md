# Access Control Policy

## Purpose
To ensure that access to systems is restricted to authorized users.

## Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party users.

## Policy
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) must be enforced
- Least privilege principle must be applied
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory

## Responsibilities
- IT Team: Implement access controls
- Employees: Protect credentials

## Enforcement
Violations may result in disciplinary action.
